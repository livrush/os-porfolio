About

Olivia Rush is a professional dancer trained in ballet, contemporary, modern, tap, and other types of dance. Liv moved from her hometown of Woodinville, Washington to attend California Institute of the Arts (CalArts), where she obtained her bachelors of fine arts in dance. As well as across the United States, Liv has performed in Russia, Germany, and South Korea. Olivia was the first CalArts student to take part in the exchange program between CalArts and Korea National University of the Arts in Seoul, South Korea.

Liv has been self studying web design for over ten years. She's gained a solid base knowledge of HTML, CSS, and begun to grasp Javascript and jQuery. Unfortunately slow, unfocused self study has resulted in poor confidence in her coding abilities, so she's decided to take the next step by pursuing Operation Spark's bootcamp.

Olivia currently resides in New Orleans, Louisiana, where she's employed as an office assistant at the New Orleans City Planning Commission. Liv is also a member of the dance company FLOCK, though she is on a personal hiatus.
